Indulge in the Local Cuisine of Buxar

Buxar is renowned for its delectable cuisine, which is a blend of traditional Bihari flavors and influences from neighboring regions. From mouthwatering street food to hearty home-cooked meals, Buxar offers a culinary experience that will tantalize your taste buds.

Litti Chokha

Litti Chokha is a popular dish in Bihar, and Buxar is no exception. Made from roasted wheat balls (litti) served with mashed potatoes, brinjal curry, and tangy chutneys, this dish is a must-try for visitors looking to savor the authentic flavors of Bihari cuisine.

Sattu Paratha

Sattu Paratha is another favorite among locals and visitors alike. Made from roasted gram flour (sattu) stuffed in wheat dough and cooked on a griddle, this wholesome and nutritious dish is often enjoyed with a dollop of ghee or yogurt.

Chaat

No culinary journey in Buxar is complete without sampling the street food delights, especially the chaat. From tangy Aloo Tikki Chaat to spicy Pani Puri, the streets of Buxar are dotted with vendors offering an array of lip-smacking chaat options that are sure to tantalize your taste buds.
