Explore the Attractions of Buxar

Buxar offers a diverse range of attractions that showcase its rich history and natural beauty. Whether you're a history enthusiast, a nature lover, or simply looking for a serene retreat, Buxar has something to offer for everyone.

Buxar Fort

The Buxar Fort, situated on the banks of the Ganges River, is a must-visit attraction for history buffs. Built during the Mughal era, the fort offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and provides insights into the city's storied past.

Gandhi Setu

The Gandhi Setu, spanning the River Ganges, is one of the longest river bridges in India. Offering breathtaking views of the river and the surrounding countryside, the bridge is a popular spot for photography enthusiasts and nature lovers.

National Martyrs Memorial

The National Martyrs Memorial, located in Buxar, commemorates the brave souls who sacrificed their lives for India's independence. Surrounded by lush greenery, the memorial provides a serene setting for reflection and remembrance.
